Play Monitoring: General Facts



With the abstract monitor-class HP_PLAY_MONITOR the library HP MIDIFILE offers an interface to listen at special events during the play of a MIDI-file. If the user wants to monitor the play, he has to create an own class which is derived from HP_PLAY_MONITOR and he has to realize the member-functions of the class with his requirements. At runtime, he has to send a pointer of an instance of his class to the library. While playing, all functions are called by the library each time, when the event happens. The member-functions allow to realize the display of takt, beat, lyrics and chords.

RPN / NRPN: General Facts



The extended controller events RPN and NRPN are compound events, i.e. these are built by four or three basic controller events. The first two, the RPN MSB resp. NRPN MSB and RPN LSB resp. NRPN LSB define the number by concatenation of two seven-bit-numbers. Similar the next two data MSB and Data LSB define the data-value by concatenation of two seven-bit-data. The data also can be defined to be relative by only one seven-bit-data: The events Data increment or the Data decrement. Here the last data-value will be incremented or decremented. The functions ending with "Relative" create or change events of the last types, the functions ending with "Absolute" create or change events with absolute data-values.

The parameters RPN- and NRPN-numbers and the RPN- and NRPN-data-values have to be built by concatenations of the seven bits MSB with the seven bits LSB.

HP MIDIFile manipulates RPN- and NRPN-events as entities. With the functions of HP MIDIFILE it is not possible to read, insert, change or delete the basic controller events of RPN and NRPN.

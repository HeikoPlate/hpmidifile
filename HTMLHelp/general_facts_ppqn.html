PPQN: General Facts



PPQN (pulses per quarter note) is a number, which indicates how many ticks (i.e. pulses or clocks) per quarter note resolution the time-stamps of the MIDI-events are based upon.

HP MIDIFILE accepts MIDI-files with all PPQN, but sets the value only with numbers 48, 72, 96, 120, 144, 168, 192, 216, 240, 360, 384, 480, 960 and 1920.

Each MIDI-file must have a PPQN. Editing a new MIDI_file without loading a file before, PPQN has to be defined first.
